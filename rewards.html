Curiosities Answered.

How do I earn NEXA Coins?

Every bill payment, UPI transaction, and card spend through NEXA generates coins. The rate depends on your Membership Tier.

Do coins expire?

Coins earned by Gold and Platinum members have no expiration. Bronze member coins expire after 12 months of inactivity.

Can I transfer coins?

Currently, coins are locked to your unique NEXA ID to ensure the security of our ecosystem.

How do I upgrade my tier?

Tier upgrades are based on your annual spending volume and bill payment consistency. Premium tiers can also be unlocked via invite.
